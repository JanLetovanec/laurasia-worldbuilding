#The Great Player
**The Great Player** is an underground organization located in **Capitol** ([[Galicia|Republic of Galicia]]). With its unique location serving as a life-blood for trade, Galician culture, and a focal point for most diplomats, artists and spies, it should come as a no surprise that Capitol became the center of **The Great Game**.

The Great Player is semi-organized group that utilizes this fact and became a prominent player of The Game - exchanging payment for influencing the world's events on your behalf.

## Specialty
The Great Player have little to no use as a criminal organization. They realize the true power of diplomacy. They know which strings to pull, which favours to cash-in and what assets to hold on to.
